Vasantha Kala Nadigalile: Feature Spoiler

 Posted on October 10, 2011  |  1 minutes  |  103 words  |  Janani Dhinakaran

Talk about twisted! Rajani is a true villian in this song. You must watch the video to understand why, though the audio alone should also suffice.

The translation is written in the video itself.

Mundru Muduchu is another one of the initial movies where Kamal was the good guy and Rajani was the bad guy. When people started realising the true potential of both actors, they stopped acting together. Rajani’s character wants Sri Devi’s but she is in love with Kamal’s character. After this song and a mourning period, she marries the widowed father of Rajani, making her his step-mum. Talk about twisted!
