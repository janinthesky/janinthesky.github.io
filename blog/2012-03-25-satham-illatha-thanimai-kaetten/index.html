Satham Illatha Thanimai Kaetten

 Posted on March 25, 2012  |  6 minutes  |  1201 words  |  Janani Dhinakaran

This song is from a movie called Amarkalam, starring Ajit and Shalini. The song is sung by SPB and supposed to have been breathless in the movie. I’m not sure this is possible in real life. In the movie Ajit has written a poem on the rock which Shalini tries to put to tune. He doesn’t like it and she asks him to sing instead, thinking he won’t be able to. I recommend you watch this video from 1:23 time point.

http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2dFfc4JbKtY
