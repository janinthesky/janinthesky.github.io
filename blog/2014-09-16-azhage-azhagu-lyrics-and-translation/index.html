Azhage Azhagu: Lyrics and Translation

 Posted on September 15, 2014  |  3 minutes  |  628 words  |  Janani Dhinakaran

This is a translation of a recent song from a movie called Saivam that my mum recommended I listen to. I listened to it last night and fell in love with the voice. The singer is the daughter of karnatic singer Unnikrishnan who has also worked on some film music himself. The song has been on repeat all day and the lyrics are so beautiful in Tamil. I hope the translation conveys some of that.

**Sa Sa Sa Sa Ni Sa Ri Sa Pa

Ma Ma Tha Tha Thasa Ri Pa Pa

Sa Sa Sa Sa Ni Sa Ri Sa Pa

Ma Ma Tha Tha Thasa Ri Pa Pa

Ga Ma Tha Ni Sa

Ni Ri Sa**

Notes

**Azhage Azhage Ethuvum Azhage

Anbin Vizhiyil Ellaam Azhage

Mazhai Mattumaa Azhagu

Sudum Veyyil Kooda Oru Azhagu

Malar Mattumaa Azhagu

Vizhum Ilai Kooda Oru Azhagu**

Beauty, Beauty, Everything has Beauty

When you look through Affection’s eyes, Everything is beautiful

Is only the rain beautiful?

Even the hot sun is beautiful

Is only the flower beautiful?

Even the falling leaf is beautiful

**Punnagai Veesidum Paarvaigal Azhagu

Vaarthaigal Theergaiyil Mounangal Azhagu

Nanmaikku Sollidum Poigalum Azhagu

Unmaiyil Adhudhaan Meyyaai Azhagu

Ga Ma Tha Ni Sa Ni Ri Sa Ga Ma Tha Ni Sa Ga Ri Sa**

The glance that throws a smile is beautiful

The silence after words run out is beautiful

The lies told for someone’s good is beautiful

Truly, that is the real beauty

For the peacock to dance

Does it need the sound of beats?

For the river to walk and flow

Does it need route guidence?

For the waves of the sea to speak

Does it need literary languages?

If you’re with nature

The whole world is beautiful

If you forget your worries,

This life is full of beauty
