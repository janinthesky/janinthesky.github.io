LfE 23: Spring in full swing

 Posted on February 18, 2012  |  1 minutes  |  148 words  |  Janani Dhinakaran

05/04/2010

Hello everyone,

We passed the Equinox on the 21st of March. We celebrated and welcomed spring in full swing. Daffodils now grow where the White and purple flowers did. The Meadows are bright with them. Children are everywhere! Easter seems like a celebration of spring- with all the bunnies and eggs. Days are getting longer and longer which is always good. It stays bright till 7:30 or so. So we feel like we have all day to get anything done.

My project is going well. Some important deadlines approaching but hope to be prepared.

It’s been a while since I last wrote. But perhaps it’s because of the routine. Parties, coffees and outings happen. PhDs – I keep applying. Doubt about the future …where I’ll be doing what in 6 months…

Live each day.

Hope you are all happy and healthy and derive strength from Spring time.
